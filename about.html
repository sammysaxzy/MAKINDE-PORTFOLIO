About Me

Get to know more about my journey and passion

"Data is the new oil, and I'm here to refine it into valuable insights."

Hello! I'm Makinde Samuel

I’m a passionate Data Analyst and Data Scientist dedicated to uncovering insights that drive informed business decisions. My journey into the world of data began with a curiosity about how information can transform organizations and solve real-world challenges.

I have successfully completed a comprehensive Data Analytics Training program at 8thGear (LSETF Sponsored) and a Data Science & Machine Learning Fellowship under the 3MTT/DeepTech (FG Sponsored) initiative. I’m currently in the mentorship phase of the 3MTT Fellowship, applying advanced techniques in Python, Power BI, and Machine Learning to real-world projects.

During my DeepTech / 3MTT Mentorship Phase, I worked on impactful data-driven projects such as building credit risk prediction models, performing feature engineering on structured and semi-structured datasets, and developing analytical workflows for business intelligence. These projects strengthened my ability to design end-to-end data solutions from data extraction and cleaning to model deployment and insight communication.

Through my internships at Elevvo and FibreHub, I’ve gained hands-on experience in data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, dashboard creation, and business intelligence reporting. I’ve worked on impactful projects involving survey data interpretation, retail performance analytics, and strategic KPI monitoring.

In addition to my analytics journey, I bring technical expertise from my role at Sparktek Communications, where I install and maintain network infrastructure, CCTV systems, and solar solutions. This technical background gives me a unique edge in understanding how data and technology integrate to improve business performance.
